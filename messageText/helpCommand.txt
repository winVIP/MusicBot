**{prefix}play** [Song query/Song link/Playlist link] - Plays the song(s) from the given name or url
**{prefix}skip** - Skips the song that is playing or if vote skip is enables, votes to skip
**{prefix}stop** - Stops what is playing and disconnects the bot
**{prefix}queue** - Shows the queue
**{prefix}clear** - Clears the queue
**{prefix}settings** - Opens the settings help menu
**{prefix}fs** - Skips the song that is playing, even if vote skip is enabled
